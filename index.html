Our Vision & Mission

TPAC Church was founded on the belief that faith should transform individuals, families, and communities. We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's love and discover their purpose.

Our mission is to make disciples who make disciples, planting churches that plant churches, and seeing entire communities transformed by the gospel of Jesus Christ.
