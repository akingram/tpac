Our Story

About TPAC Church

Rooted in faith. Grounded in love. Called to transform.

Our Journey

A Legacy of
Transforming Faith

TPAC Church was planted in 2008 from a small gathering of believers in a living room with a single vision: to see lives, families, and communities transformed by the power of the Gospel.

Today, we stand as a vibrant congregation of over 500 members, reaching thousands more through our digital ministry and Telegram channel, fulfilling our calling to make disciples of all nations.

2008

Founded as a home church group by Pastor James & Sarah Wilson

2011

Moved to our first dedicated building; grew to 150 members

2015

Launched digital ministry; first Telegram channel for sermon distribution

2018

Expanded to current main auditorium; surpassed 500 members

2023

3,000+ Telegram subscribers; 24 active missions partnerships worldwide

What We Stand For

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To see entire communities transformed by the Gospel — individuals healed, families restored, and society shaped by Kingdom values.

Our Mission

Making disciples who make disciples — planting churches that plant churches, and walking with every believer from salvation to full maturity in Christ.

Core Values

Love without limits. Authenticity over performance. Generosity as a lifestyle. Excellence in all things. Unity in the Spirit across every background and culture.

Leadership

Meet Our Pastors

Pastor James Wilson

Senior Pastor & Founder

A visionary leader with over 20 years of pastoral experience. Pastor James founded TPAC with a heart to see the unreached hear the Good News. He holds a Masters in Theology and is a sought-after conference speaker.

Pastor Sarah Wilson

Associate Pastor & Co-Founder

A gifted teacher and counselor, Pastor Sarah leads our women's ministry and discipleship programs. Her warmth and wisdom create the welcoming culture TPAC is known for. She is also an author of two devotional books.

Pastor David Osei

Youth & Missions Pastor

Passionate about the next generation, Pastor David leads our vibrant youth ministry and coordinates our 24 active missions partnerships. A dynamic communicator with a heart for the unreached.
