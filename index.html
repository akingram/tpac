Our Story

About The Purpose Place

Rooted in faith. Grounded in love. Called to raise and equip men for purposeful living.

Our Journey

A Legacy of
Transforming Faith

The Purpose Place Apostolic Church was established with a clear and powerful vision: to be a Christ-centered community that raises and equips men for purposeful living. Based in Ado Ekiti, we are passionate about living out God's purposes and making disciples who obey the commands of Christ.

Through our apostolic and prophetic mandate, we help identify God's calling in every life and raise a prospect end-time army — grounded in Exodus 18:20 and Matthew 28:19–20.

Leadership

Meet Our Pastoral Team

Apostle Akin-Akinyemi

Senior Pastor & Apostolic Oversight

A seasoned minister of the Gospel, an Apostle with a prophetic grace, whose love for God is contagious — inspiring young ones on living purposefully. He is the apostolic oversight for The Purpose Place Apostolic Church, a Graduate of Ekiti State University and The ECWA Theological Seminary where he holds a PGD in Theology. He is happily married.

Blessing Akin-Akinyemi
